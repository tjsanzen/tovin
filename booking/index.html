What to expect

First a little about me; My name is Tovin, I'm a photographer, as well as other things, and I like taking pictures of beautiful things, many of which just happen to be people! So, many people are nervous when getting their picture taken, and I try to make it feel like you're just being normal, and having a good time, while someone takes pictures of you. I have done family pictures, portraits, as well as engagement photos in the past, and I would be super excited to work with you! Sadly, because some people aren't the nicest always, I have a contract so that you know exactly what you will and won't get from a photo shoot, how and if you'll have to credit me, how soon you'll get your pictures, and what you can do with those pictures! Just a warning, I'm a pretty chill guy and so you don't have to worry.
