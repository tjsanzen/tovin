ABOUT ME

I Wanna Make A Difference.

I think on some level, we all want to make a difference. We want people to look back on us after we're gone and say, "Wow. They were amazing." What we have to do to have people say that, is different for all of us. For some of us, it might be becoming famous. For others, inventing a new car, or building a new phone. And yet for others, it might be as simple as smiling at our neighbor, or buying a coffee for the person behind you at Starbucks. For me, I want to spread the love of Jesus, and I'm trying to do this through Speed the Light. Speed the Light is a Christian organization that works with youth in the United States to raise money, and then use that money to send missionaries with Bibles for people who don't have access to one in other countries, to buy sound systems and transportation (Airplanes, cars, vans, motorcycles, bicycles, trucks, buses, boats, horses, camels, donkeys, wheel barrows, etc...) for missionaries, and to rescue people from the grips of sin, and to let them know that there is hope in the darkness.
Simply put, I'm helping so our missionaries can travel faster, preach clearer, and be heard louder in order for all to know Jesus.
