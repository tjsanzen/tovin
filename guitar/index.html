About Me!

I am a 17 year old Worship Pastor and Indie Pop Artist from rural Minnesota! When I'm not making music or working, I like to spend my free time doing photography, working out, and cooking!

Musical Background

I have quite a few years of musical experience! I started taking piano lessons back in 2009, and quickly progressed until I no longer needed them. I picked up guitar in 2015, and since then have also branced out to other instruments, including drums, kalimba, ukelele, irish tin whistle, various synths, and more! My training also includes two years of musical theory, a year of choral experience, and experience with countless live preformances.
