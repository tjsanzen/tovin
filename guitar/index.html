About Me!

I am a 17 year old Worship Pastor and Pop Indie Artist from rural Minnesota! When I'm not making music or working, I like to spend my free time doing photography, working out, and cooking!

Musical Background

I have quite a few years of musical experience! I started taking piano lessons back in 2009, and quickly progressed until I no longer needed them. I picked up guitar in 2015, and since then have also branched out to other instruments, including drums, kalimba, ukelele, irish tin whistle, various synths, and more! My training also includes two years of musical theory, a year of choral experience, and experience with countless live preformances.

Rates

Sadly, as much as I'd love to, I can't give guitar lessons for free, because I have a strong passion for being able to afford food. However, I try and make it as affordable as possible for you! I start at $30 for a half hour lesson, plus $2 per mile that I have to drive to the location of the lesson. See my rates page for more information on this!
